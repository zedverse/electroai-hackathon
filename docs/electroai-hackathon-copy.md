## Revised Core Copy

**Subject: Win prizes + build real engineering AI — ElectroAI Hackathon (6–10 April)**

Hi everyone,

**Build AI Agents. Solve Real Engineering Problems. Produce output to IEEE standards.**

Join the ElectroAI Hackathon from 6–10 April 2026 and work on a genuine engineering challenge using the latest AI agent tools.

**Your challenge:**
Use AI agents to transform engineering diagrams — electrical schematics, hydraulic circuits, scanned PDFs, and screenshots — into structured, simulation-ready outputs that can be used in real engineering tools such as Proteus, LTspice, and KiCad. This is a practical engineering problem, not a generic AI task.

**Your time commitment:**
One hour per team member per day, Monday 6th to Friday 10th April.

**What you'll get:**

- Prizes for top teams
- Entries judged by expert Electrical Engineer IEEE judges
- Expert mentorship to get you up to speed on the freshest Agentic AI tools
- Baseline solution to get you going
- Hands-on workshops on AI agents, vision-language models, and agentic workflows
- A research paper written to IEEE publication standard — real academic output that will further your academic and professional career
- Transferable skills that help you tackle a wide range of problems
- The chance to meet students across engineering, computing, and STEM, and Electrical Engineers from Industry and Academia

Late arrivals welcome — join us for the coding allnighter April 9th (pizza + monster), fine tune or start from scratch, ready for presenting Friday 10

Whether you are new to AI or already building, this is a chance to develop practical skills, work on a technically serious challenge, and produce something that matters academically and professionally.

**6–10 April 2026 City St George's, University of London**

**ElectroAI Hackathon — 6–10 April 2026**
